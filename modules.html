OUR MODULES

HOME

These modules help in the smooth functioning of Library Management System

Cataloguing

It is the process of creating a list of all bibliographic items such as short description, list of subjects, author name and the classification.

Membership Module

This module keep track of all important information of student, who has taken membership of library.

Report Generator

It helps in generating various reports like status of issued books, total members, books returned etc.

Circulation

It is a central and highly visible function of library, as it keeps the record of status of books in the library.

OPAC

This module helps in maintaining and organizing the database of the library. Keeping record of each and every item in library.

Transaction Management

Transaction is managing of all books, magazines, journals in the library for fast functioning of software.
