Introduction

The Library Management System is a web-based application designed to help libraries efficiently manage their resources, including books, journals, and multimedia materials. It provides librarians with tools to organize, catalog, and track library items, as well as manage patron information and circulation activities.

Features

  • Book Cataloging and Inventory Management
  • Patron Management and User Authentication
  • Circulation Management (Check-in, Check-out)
  • Reservation and Hold Requests
  • Reporting and Analytics
